WE ARE COOKIES
ABOUT US
Choreo Cookies was founded in 2002 by Eddie Macaranas and Emmett Agapay in Oceanside, California. With no aspirations of ever entering the dance community, this close-knit group of friends rehearsed for a few years before they had the opportunity to perform in front of their first live audience at the 2004 Full Out Showcase. From then on, they became regular performers at this annual show. In 2007, Choreo Cookies merged with Studio 429 and decided to reestablish itself as a competitive dance company. Since then, Choreo Cookies has been regular competitors in many of the major dance competitions including Prelude/The Bridge, Fusion, Ultimate Brawl, World of Dance, Body Rock, etc.
